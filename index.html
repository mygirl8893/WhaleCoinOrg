Roadmap

Here are a few of our milestones since August 2017
and a look at what we have planned for the future.

Pre - 200,000 Block

Pre-Launch

• Created hyperfast difficulty adjustment algorithm (Ahab)
• Created unique reward structure with Social Reward fund
• Released Whitepaper and BCT announcement
• Launched website and social media for WhaleCoin

Genesis Block

(Sep 4, 2017)

• Launched with 1 coin per block reward to limit incentives and test Ahab
• Ramped up to reward of 15 coins per block at block 1,000

Post-Launch

• Facilitated creation of WhaleCoin mining pools
• WhaleCoin Block Explorer
• Web Wallet and Desktop Wallet
• Facilitated Exchange Integration for WhaleCoin
• Relaunched WhaleCoin Website

Post - 200,000 Block

Launch Social Network

Nov 2017 - Jan 2018

• Nov 6, 2017 - Preview version of social network released where Whales earn ⅓ of mining rewards
• Nov 20, 2017 - Preview version updated with block limits on how often Whales can claim rewards
• Dec 25, 2017 - Social rewards network released with rewards for Moderators and Followers
• Jan 2018 - Social network capabilities integrated with existing wallets, tutorials created

Post-Launch

Spring - Summer 2018

• May 31, 2018 - Hardfork to redistribute mining rewards, giving more to miners and social network
• TBD - Ethereum checkpoint anchoring to protect against 51% attacks
• TBD - Continue to adjust network mechanics to increase social interactions
• TBD - Improve UX Design of social network and dashboards
• TBD - Launch WhaleCoin plugins for popular social networks
